Panoramic Photographs by Ron Meisel
The panoramic photographs were taken with a Hasselblad Xpan camera, Fuji color negative film, and Epson scanner; printed on Epson 4800 printer with Epson Ultrachrome K3 inks; and printed on Hahnemuhle Photo Rag Bright White 310 g/m archival paper.

RON MEISEL is a member of the Soho Photo Gallery, NY and a co-chair of its National Photography Competition. He had a solo show, Greetings from Coney Island, Panoramic Photographs, at Phyllis Stigliano Gallery in 2007. His work is in permanent collections including the Brooklyn Historical Society, Brooklyn, NY; The Brooklyn Collection, Brooklyn Public Library, Central, Brooklyn, NY; Brooklyn Public Library, Coney Island Branch, NY; International Center of Photography, NY; Library of Congress, Washington, DC; New-York Historical Society, NY; and U.S. Bank, Corporate Collection, NY.
